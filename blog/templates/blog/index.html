About Me

Hi! My name is David Scheuermann, and I’m a 26-year-old software developer with a passion for computers, learning, movies, video games, and food. I’ve lived in the New Orleans area all my life, and I am currently attending LSU to finish a degree in Computer Science. I built this website to showcase my abilities with HTML and CSS as well as my understanding of the Django web framework and, thus, Python. I wanted to show that I was capable in both back-end and front-end software development, though I prefer to work on the back-end. I can program in C++, C#, Python, Java, C, and JavaScript, and I feel comfortable learning any new language or framework if needed. I have a decent understanding of HTML5 and CSS3, and I’ve done some front-end work with Angular 2, Bootstrap, and Semanti UI. I also have a decent understanding of SQL (PostgreSQL specifically) and MongoDB. I love open source software and especially the Linux operating system, and I’m currently diving into video game development, as I’ve discovered a passion for it. I’m a big movie buff, loving films from all times and of various genres. I have a problem buying Criterion Collection Blu Rays each time there is a sale. I have a particular fondness for Star Wars which I watch at least once a year. I love video games, with Shadow of the Colossus and Mass Effect being my two favorites, but I’ve been playing them since I had Super Mario Bros. 3 on the NES. I’m a big Saints fan, a Liverpool FC fan, and a Pelicans fan. And I love trying new foods and seeing new places. Thanks for visiting my website!
