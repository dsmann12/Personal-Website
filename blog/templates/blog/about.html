What's a David Scheuermann?

Good question.

I’m a 26 year old software engineer with a passion for computers, learning, movies, video games, and food.

I’ve lived in the New Orleans area all my life, and I am currently attending LSU to finish a degree in Computer Science.

I built this website to showcase my abilities with HTML and CSS as well as my understanding of the Django web framework and, thus, Python. I wanted to show that I was capable in both back-end and front-end software development, though I prefer to work on the back-end.

I have been interested in computers and technology since I was in grade school, and I finally began learning programming when I started college.

I started my programming education by learning Java and object-oriented design. Eventually, I expanded to learn C++, C, C#, Python, and JavaScript, and I feel comfortable learning any new language or framework if needed.

I’ve also gained a decent understanding of HTML5 and CSS3, allowing me to create and design web pages. I’ve built up my front-end knowledge, leaning to use the Angular 2, Bootstrap, and Semantic UI (which is used by this blog) to make nice-looking interfaces.

I’m comfortable with a few web frameworks. This website was built with Django, as stated, but I’ve also developed a website with the MEAN stack, using Node.js and Express.js, and I have done some work using ASP.NET. I’ve also developed desktop GUI applications using Qt and WPF in .NET.

I have a decent understanding of SQL, preferring PostgreSQL over other flavors. However, I also have some experience with MongoDB.

I’ve begun experimenting with video game development, starting with simple web games with HTML5 and JavaScript and recently moving on to learn Unity. I have some examples of what I’ve done on this page.

I love open source software, and I’ve been running Linux on my laptops for nearly ten years (usually as a dual boot option). Each year I gain a better understanding of the operating system, and I’m pretty comfortable writing shell scripts in Bash. Android is my mobile operating system of choice for similar reasons, though I’d love for a true open source, Linux-based operating system to catch on in the mobile space.

With the technical stuff out of the way now, here’s a little more about me personally.

I love movies, old and new, foreign and domestic, color and black and white. I have a problem with buying Criterion Collection Blu Rays, and I’m always willing to catch the latest critically acclaimed films.

I have a particular fondness for Star Wars. It’s one of the first films I remember seeing in theaters, having been taken to see the special edition in 1997. I watch the original trilogy at least once per year

I have a deep appreciation for video games, which I consider my favorite entertainment medium. I’ve been playing them since the NES with Super Mario Bros. 3.

My love of video games is what spurred me to develop my video game review web application, Logd. I wanted to keep track of what games I played and how I rated them. My favorite games can be seen in a list on this website here. I don’t know if anything will ever supplant Shadow of the Colossus and Mass Effect as my top two.

I’ve always been into sports.

Of course, I have a passion for the Saints. I’ve been cheering for them my entire life, and their Super Bowl win is one of my favorite memories ever. I spent that night walking through the French Quarter, high-fiving strangers and yelling “Who dat!” at the top of my lungs.

I’ve grown extremely interested in soccer since 2012, supporting Liverpool FC in the Premier League. I also enjoy basketball and go to Pelicans games whenever I can (although I have to admit I enjoy watching the Warriors play as well).

I’d like to gain a better appreciation of music, although I am open minded when it comes to genre. My favorite artists are Radiohead, Burial, Aphex Twin, The Beatles, and Run The Jewels.

I love trying new restaurants and eating new foods. I’m always willing to try anything once, no matter how weird it might be.

I’m really big on traveling, hoping to one day step foot on every continent.

Thanks for visiting my website!
